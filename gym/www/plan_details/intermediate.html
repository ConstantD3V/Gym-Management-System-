Intermediate Plan Details

Workout Plan

Our Intermediate Plan is designed for those with some experience in fitness, incorporating moderate-intensity workouts to build muscle and endurance.

  • 4x per week: Upper/lower body split (strength training)
  • 40-minute cardio sessions (running, rowing, or HIIT)
  • Stretching, mobility, and core strengthening exercises

Diet Plan

This plan emphasizes balanced macros with increased protein intake to support muscle recovery and growth.

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with whole wheat toast and avocado
  • Lunch: Grilled turkey, brown rice, and steamed vegetables
  • Snack: Protein smoothie with banana and almond butter
