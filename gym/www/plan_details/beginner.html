Beginner Plan Details

Workout Plan

Our Beginner Plan includes simple, low-intensity workouts to help you get started. These exercises focus on building strength and improving cardiovascular fitness.

  • 3x per week: Full body workout (strength training)
  • 30-minute cardio sessions (walking or cycling)
  • Stretching and mobility exercises

Diet Plan

Our diet plan focuses on balanced nutrition, including proteins, carbs, and healthy fats to fuel your body while maintaining a calorie deficit.

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with fruits and nuts
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken, quinoa, and vegetables
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with seeds and berries
  • Dinner: Salmon, sweet potatoes, and steamed broccoli
